About Us

Fresno Farmers Market

Fresno has long been known as one of the largest agricultural sites in California and it isn't only limited to exporting those goods to other states. Fresno has this trend of setting up a physical retail marketplace intended to sell foods directly by farmers to consumers. In Fresno, outdoor markets are on set on different days every week on a set schedule, mainly on Riverpark, Valley Childrens Hospital, Old Town Clovis, and many more.
